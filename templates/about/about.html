Our Story

Background

Josie Anne’s Patisserie was founded by Elaine Meade in 2020.

Elaine has loved baking from a very early age. She grew up in a small village in Clare, baking with her Granny Josie. Granny Josie taught Elaine how to make mouth watering queen cakes, melt in the mouth apple tarts and buttery scones. They spent hours in the kitchen every day, with Granny Josie finessing Elaine’s baking skills. Elaine was also inspired by her Granny Anne, who owned and ran her own restaurant in Carrick-on-Suir. She showed Elaine that through hard work and consistently delicious food – anything is possible!

When Elaine set up her own business in 2020, she was determined to honour both Granny Anne and Granny Josie is the best way she knew how! Hence Josie Anne’s Patisserie was born!

About us

Elaine qualified from Galway, Mayo Institute of Technology in 2015 with Culinary Arts and Business degree. Through this degree Elaine has worked in some of Ireland and the UKs most prestigious establishments, including Adare Manor and The Dorchester Hotel.

Upon completing this degree, Elaine moved to London. During her time in London, she worked with world renowned pastry chefs in some of the most innovative and forward-thinking pastry event companies around London.

Josie Anne’s Patisserie is an online platform where customers can order sweet treats, afternoon tea, bespoke cakes, tarts, scones and a wide variety of desserts. These are available for click and collect, or local delivery. Bespoke treat boxes are also available for nationwide delivery.
